Call For Sessions

La Call For Sessions è aperta fino alla sera di domenica 22 aprile.

Il movimento agile è ormai quasi maggiorenne.
Nel tempo l'approccio basato su valori e principi agili ha mantenuto la sua freschezza e validità.
È stato utilizzato con successo, sia in termini di mindset che di pratiche, non solo da parte di organizzazioni squisitamente software, ma anche in ambito hardware e per progetti in cui un elevato e sofisticato mix di entrambi richiede lo sviluppo di prodotti ad elevata complessità.

Questa edizione di “mini Agile Day” si pone, una volta di più, l'obiettivo di condividere ed esplorare tali metodologie, sia che si tratti di “good practices” applicate all'ambiente di sviluppo, sia che si tratti di modelli organizzativi concretamente realizzati, oppure ancora oggetto di ricerca.

Keynote

Evolve or Die: The Changing Role of the Enterprise Architect in the Modern Age Thomas Glover

What is the role of the modern Enterprise Architect in the age of agile software development, continuous delivery, Infrastructure as Code and Evolutionary Architecture? We look at the highly evolving demands in Enterprise Architecture from the perspective of the innovative world of the Internet of Things (IoT). How can we balance innovation and strategic governance? The answer lies in our remarkable ability as humans to adapt to change.

Thomas Glover

Tom is a highly experienced and educated Mobile Enterprise Architect with expertise gained almost 20 years across a varied range of industry sectors, including Telecommunications, Healthcare, Financial Services and Utilities. He has extensive knowledge and experience in emerging technologies and innovation and is a recognised thought-leader in RFID, M2M, Internet Of Things (IoT) and tracking and traceability.
Tom is a well-respected Enterprise Architect with a considerable technology background covering many industry verticals, notably in the area of Mobility Strategy and Architecture. In addition to the scope of his experience, he has a proven capability to understand the complex technical aspects of the solutions he is working with.

Tom is a keen evangelist in the use of proven innovation for Enterprise Architectural modelling and Architectural Description, notably in the area of The Open Group ArchiMate open modelling standard.

Tom possesses considerable experience in the areas of Emerging Technologies and Innovation. He has worked with large corporate customers on various engagements to design and implement Proof-of-Concept capability for emerging technologies such as Internet of Things,RFID, M2M and Location Based Services.
